🚉 Metropark Station — Iselin, NJ
Best park-and-ride in NJ for NYC visitors.
Covered multi-story garage, 24 hrs, ~$10–11/day. ~45 min to NYC Penn Station/Moynihan Train Hall via NJ Transit Northeast Corridor Line.

Do before you go: Download the NexPass app — registers your plate so you just drive in/out. No ticket to lose. Confirm current rates at nexusparkingsystems.com.
🗽 Getting Around NYC
All core stops cluster within a 2-mile Midtown radius — walkable between most. Use Uber for Chelsea Market (further south) and anything after 10 PM. Avoid the subway late at night; Uber is the right call for two women solo after dark. Budget ~$15–25 per Uber trip within Midtown.
🚆 NJ Transit — How to Ride
Line: Northeast Corridor Line — direct, no transfers, Metropark → NYC Penn Station/Moynihan Hall (~43 min avg).

Frequency: Every 20 minutes. First train 12:05 AM, last from Metropark 11:17 PM. They will never be stranded.

Fare: ~$7–16/person one-way (peak vs. off-peak). Budget ~$15/person each way.

Buy tickets: Download the free NJ Transit app before leaving Fort Wayne. Create an account. Purchase through MyTix in the app — displays on phone, no paper needed. Select: Northeast Corridor → Metropark → New York Penn Station (or reverse). Don't activate the ticket until boarding.

Critical: Amtrak trains share the same tracks — NJ Transit tickets are NOT valid on Amtrak. The app's DepartureVision screen shows which departing trains are NJ Transit vs. Amtrak. Always board the NJ Transit train.
🔙 June 24 Return
Check out, Uber to Penn Station, NJ Transit back to Metropark. Aim to be at Penn Station by 10–11 AM to clear NJ before afternoon traffic builds. Back in Fort Wayne by 9–10 PM. Avoid departing after noon — NJ/PA weekend traffic is brutal by 1 PM.

Peace of Mind
Safety Notes
🏙️ Neighborhood Reality
The entire itinerary — Times Square, 5th Ave, Rockefeller, Central Park, Herald Square, Grand Central — is in one of the most heavily policed tourist corridors on earth. Extremely safe during the day and evening. No concerns for this itinerary as planned.
🌙 After Dark
Times Square and Midtown are well-lit and busy until midnight. Stick to main streets, not side alleys after 10 PM. Uber over walking for anything after 10 PM, especially to/from Chelsea which is further from Midtown.
🚕 Transportation
Use Uber or Lyft exclusively. Avoid hailing random cabs. The app shows driver name, plate, and route — much safer. Share trip status with Jon from the Uber app so he can track in real time.
📱 Stay Connected
